What is Resident Evil 4? 

In this game, players take the role of a US government special agent named Leon S. Kennedy. Players are sent on a mission to bring Ashley Graham, the US president’s daughter from the hands of kidnappers. Set in the rural ambiance of Europe, lean combats hordes of villagers who are diseased by a mind-controlling parasite. Ultimately at the end of this mission and struggle, he reunited with his long-lost spy Ada Wong. 

The game diverts from the conventional tight pathways, city streets to meet the high expectations of players and critics. The world of the European village wouldn’t bore you to any extent. Through your journey, you would come across the castle, old ruins, mines, local people. Interesting every location in the game is different from each other. No matter its spring or autumnal touch, the villagers would render a constant feeling of horror and terror in the minds of the players. 

Gameplay

As we said earlier, players take the role of Leon S. Kennedy from the third-person perspective. Unlike the previous sequels of this game, Resident Evil 4 highly concentrates on shootouts, action, and violence. Unlike its previous games, players have more options other than only shooting straight, down, or up. Shootings also have more effects on enemies. For example, fire shot to the feet would cause one to stumble. Shots aimed at the arms can make enemies drop their weapons. 

The main enemies of the village are known as Los Ganados, meaning the cattle. Leon fights against these ganados throughout the game mainly. These local people as depicted in the game are extremely strong and fierce. They are collectively capable of working and communicating with one another and at the same time can dodge, launch melee attacks and projectile weapons. 

People can enjoy Resident Evil 4 in high definition on PlayStation with an enhanced frame rate. This game is the first of its kind, especially in the franchise to introduce the third person over the shoulder perspective. The storyline focuses on the after events that took place post the umbrella corporation destruction. With an intense storyline, featuring action and horror, the game changed the future course of Resident Evil games and influenced the plot of several other games. 

Features

Following are some of the features of this amazing game, which would give you an idea what will you get in this game and will keep you intrigued. 

  • Variety of new weapons

One can see vivid weapons available to players. Unlike the earlier games in the series, Resident Evil 4 introduces new elements to inflict damage and destruction in the game. Now, the game focuses on overpowering your enemies rather than escaping from them. One can use a variety of rifles, handguns, shotguns, etc. at their convenience. 

  • Changes in camera angles 

In Resident Evil 4, there has been a major shift in camera angles and movement of the camera system. Now, the camera gives a close view of Leon from his shoulder top. All his ballistic weapons are given a laser angle for aiming. 

  • Context-sensitive controls

The game has also introduced concept sensitive controls. This means that players can now interact with their surroundings as and when required. They can kick down a ladder, jump out of a window, dodging an enemy attack, and many more. They can even launch a melee attack against the energy while it’s crouching.

Resident Evil 4 is a revolutionary masterpiece that marks a turning point for its succeeding sequels. This is the first game in the series to introduce a third-person perspective.
